Step 2 · Educate

Not all cards cost the same

A debit card costs almost nothing to accept. A premium rewards or cashback card can cost you 3–4x more — because the bank is funding that cardholder's rewards out of your margin. Most processors blend all of this into one flat rate so you never see it. We don't.

Terms, plainly

The four words that explain your statement

Wholesale rate
The actual cost the bank charges to move money, before any markup.
Effective rate
Your total processing cost divided by your total volume — the real number, not the advertised one.
Interchange
The fee set by Visa/Mastercard/etc. per card type. This is what makes "good" and "bad" cards cost differently.
Blended rate
What Square and Stripe charge: one number covering all card types, so the spread is hidden inside it.
